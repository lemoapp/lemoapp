Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open an account with Vanguard Royal Bank?

Opening an account is simple. Visit our website or any branch near you, provide the necessary documents, and follow the guided process.

What types of accounts do you offer?

We offer savings accounts, current accounts, fixed deposit accounts, and more to suit your personal and business needs.

How can I check my account balance?

You can check your account balance through our online banking portal, mobile app, or by visiting any of our branches.

What should I do if I forget my online banking password?

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" option on our login page and follow the instructions to reset it.

Are my deposits insured?

Yes, all deposits with Vanguard Royal Bank are insured according to regulatory requirements, ensuring your money is safe with us.

Can I apply for a loan online?

Yes, you can apply for a loan through our website or mobile app. Simply log in, navigate to the loans section, and fill out the application form.

How can I update my personal information?

You can update your personal information by logging into your account and visiting the "Profile Settings" section, or by contacting customer service.

What are your transaction fees?

Our transaction fees vary depending on the type of account and transaction. Visit our fee schedule on the website for more details.

How do I report a lost or stolen debit card?

If your debit card is lost or stolen, immediately contact our customer care center to block the card and prevent unauthorized transactions.

How can I transfer money to another account?

You can transfer money using our online banking platform, mobile app, or by visiting any of our branches.

Do you offer credit cards?

Yes, we offer a variety of credit cards tailored to different financial needs. Visit our credit card section for more information and application details.

What should I do if I notice unauthorized transactions?

If you notice unauthorized transactions, contact our fraud department immediately for assistance.
